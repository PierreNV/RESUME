Hi and welcome on my resume.

My name is Pierre and this page is intended to demonstrate some of my basic html knowledge. Feel free to navigate through, I hope you will find it friendly while I focus more on the performance than the style.

I have been through a path of training modules for professional front-end developers provided by codewithmosh.com including lessons, best practice tips and exercices with challenges that I could face in real work situation.

On the next tab that you can access via the navigation bar, you will find my actual resume with my overall professional experience. You will also find a link to a dynamic React app called movly that I developed for practicing the essentials of React. Some of the programming choices here are not the most efficient options but deliberate to experiment the different aspects React has to offer. For instance, I chose to use both class and functional components for training since I would likely have to maintain existing apps with classes in the future.
